Frequently Asked Questions

You can ask your question via our email id. Frequently asked questions will be posted in this website so that you can follow us and clear your doubts

  • What is the eligibility criteria?

    Anyone with good reflex and dedication can join our org. There will be prelims and 1v1 matches with our co-leaders to add you.

  • Is a gamers future completely safe in your hands?

    Unlike semi-professional clan , we wont be signing a player for a period. If you are'nt performing good or we get to see any other better player than you, we would go for them. So if you wanna stay here , you will have to perform well.

  • How much can we earn by gaming?

    If you have a good passion in gaming you wont be looking for how much will you earn. Anyways, we will setup streaming setup for you and everything you need. you will have some stipend + bonus in the tournaments

  • Is gaming the future of India?

    This is a very higher order question. But yes, Gaming is a very fast growing sector in India. Gaming indusrty has already conquered in the other parts of the world. Here the elder people might not be used to gaming. But the future generation will.

  • What made you start this clan?

    We got to know that there is a big opportunity in this sector and we can make good use of talents of kids who are not so good at academics but in gamings. This will be an opportunity for them to prove themselves . Also we provide good network and devices for the talented players to bring up thier maximum potential which they wont get at home. We can also provide coaching for them , which will open up an opportunity for being the best in the world. In gaming sector we can have many sponsors and earn so much by streaming and winning tournaments.
